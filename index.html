Frequently Asked Questions

What is your primary investment criteria? +
Gotham Lenders focuses on non-performing residential and commercial loans where there is a clear path to resolution through restructuring or strategic asset sales. We prefer portfolios ranging from $5M to $50M.
How quickly can Gotham Lenders close on a deal? +
We pride ourselves on institutional speed. Upon receipt of a complete loan tape and initial due diligence, we can provide indicative pricing within 48 hours and closing within 10-14 business days.
What asset classes do you specialize in? +
Our expertise spans multi-family residential, commercial retail, and light industrial properties. We look for assets in primary and secondary markets with strong underlying value.
How do you handle maturity defaults? +
We act as a bridge partner. By acquiring the note from the existing lender, we can offer the borrower a tailored extension and restructuring plan that provides the time needed for a traditional refinance.
What is the "Tape" submission process? +
Institutions can submit loan tapes securely through our contact portal. Our team typically provides an initial indicative bid within 48 to 72 hours of data verification.
